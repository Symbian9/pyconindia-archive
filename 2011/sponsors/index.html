PyCon India 2011 Sponsorship

The Indian Python Software Society (IPSS) and the PyCon India organizing committee is looking for corporate sponsors to support PyCon India 2011 conference.

Your sponsorship helps keep PyCon India affordable and accessible to the widest possible audience. The conference is the largest Python Software developer conference in India.

The IPSS is a non-profit organization established to promote the use of the Python programming language. One of its main activities is to conduct the annual conference.

PyCon India 2011 will bring together Python developers and enthusiasts of various backgrounds. Companies and corporates that use Python are an integral part of the community and we welcome your patronage. By sponsoring the conference your organization will be better connected to the Indian Python community.

The tiers and benefits are listed below. For more details, please contact contact@in.pycon.org.
