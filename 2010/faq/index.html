I am confused about the various payment options available for the registration.

To make it convenient to most of (if not all) the people attending the event we have facilitated various payment methods:

The payment options (from best to worst) are

  • Pay online
  • Drop a cheque made out to IPSS at the nearest AXIS bank booth/branch
  • Pay at a local user group meeting.(Contact your Local User Group for details)
  • Pay at the venue (if you opt for this you are likely going to miss the keynotes etc.)
