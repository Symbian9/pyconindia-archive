Developing Voice Biometrics applications

by VINAY MODI (speaking)

Tweet
Section
Workshops
Technical level
Intermediate

Objective

The basic idea of this workshop is to demonstrate how to use voice biometrics api for developing novel applications.

Description

In this workshop, we will develop a sample application using InCights Voice Biometrics APIs.

1) At the beginning, there will be a brief introduction of voice biometrics.
2) In the middle, i.e. majority of the time, we will write code to develop a sample application.
3) At the end, will open the house for bouncing off ideas to encourage the developers to develop voice biometrics applications which they may like to offer to their customers.

Requirements

Python 2.7
Linux - I will be on Ubuntu 12.04 (Debian, Fedora, CentOS should work fine)

Speaker bio

Vinay is CTO and Chief Product Officer at InCights. He has several years of experience in using Python for developing software. He completed his masters in AI from The University of Edinburgh and has been working in Voice, Search Engines and Semantic Technologies ever since.

For further details, kindly visit: http://www.linkedin.com/in/vinaymodi
